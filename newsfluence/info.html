How it works:

The probability of your posts being liked or re-tweeted is based on a variety of human and non-human factors, and I believe, using a super-secret highly-advanced algorithm, to have uncovered those that are most important, producing a composite predictor score for news-sharing success. The criteria are as follows:

  • If the news has already been broken by someone else in your network.
  • The time of day you post.
  • The mood of your story (happier = better!).
  • If it matches the interests of a majority of your friends / followers.

I take each of these factors into account for each post, which in-turn allows you to make informed sharing decisions. See for yourself!
